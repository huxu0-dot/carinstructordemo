With 14 years of professional instruction experience, Conor Byrne has built a reputation as one of Dublin's most trusted and effective driving instructors.

FAQ

Answers to Common Questions

What do I need before my first lesson?
You'll need a valid Irish Learner Permit (category B). If you don't have one yet, you'll need to pass your Driver Theory Test first and then apply at your local NDLS office. Bring your permit to each lesson — Conor will verify it before you drive.
How long are lessons and what areas do you cover?
Standard lessons are 60 minutes. Conor covers all of South Dublin, Dún Laoghaire, Rathfarnham, Tallaght, Ballsbridge, and surrounding areas. Pickup and drop-off at your home, work, or college is included in the lesson price.
How many lessons will I need?
This varies for every student. Most beginners are test-ready within 10–20 lessons. After your initial assessment, Conor will give you an honest, realistic estimate. There's never any pressure to book more lessons than you need.
Do you offer weekend or evening lessons?
Yes — lessons are available 7 days a week from 7:00am to 8:00pm. Conor works around your schedule, not the other way around. Weekend and early morning slots are popular, so booking in advance is recommended.
What is the EDT (Essential Driver Training)?
The EDT is a mandatory structured programme for all learner drivers in Ireland. It consists of 12 lessons covering essential driving skills as defined by the RSA. Conor is fully certified to deliver all 12 EDT topics and logs your progress in the RSA's official system after every session.
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for a minimum of 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a lesson. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged at half price. We completely understand that life happens — emergencies are always handled with flexibility and understanding.
Can I use your car for my driving test?
Yes — using a familiar, dual-control vehicle for your test is highly recommended and a proven confidence booster. Conor will collect you, do a final 60-minute warm-up session before your test, accompany you to the test centre, and wait to celebrate your pass with you.
